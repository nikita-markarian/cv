Nikita Markarian

I am interested in the following subjects:
  • derived and higher categories;
  • Hochschild homology and cohomology, cyclic homology;
  • factorization homology;
  • little discs operad and other operads;
  • multiple zeta values and the Grothendieck-Teichmuller group;
  • Feigin-Odesski Poisson structures and other Poisson structures.
